My Hobbies

Dance

I think that dance is the most beautiful art that exists.The irony is that while dancing, every muscle of your body is under your control yet uncontrollable.Whenever you dance the world just seems to fade away, the music drowns the noise around you and you feel whole. With its therapeutic powers, I believe it is a healer of both body and soul.






Listening to Music

Music has a way of opening our hearts and helping us feel more connected to ourselves. When the music flows through me it drowns out all the noise around and I become aware of myself in an unimaginable way.

Reading

Reading has been shown to put our brains into a pleasurable trance-like state, similar to meditation, and it brings the same health benefits of deep relaxation and inner calm. Regular readers sleep better, have lower stress levels, higher self-esteem, and lower rates of depression than non-readers.It gives you somewhere to go when you have to stay in the same place.
